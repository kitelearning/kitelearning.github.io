Angie's List

Angie's List is a service (on the internet) that allows users to post jobs and talents in order to find clients. This page will give you a breif introduction to Angie's List.

Pricing

Angie's List plans starts at $3.25 per month plus a $5 startup fee, or you can choose a yearly plan that waives the fee. Paying for the $29 yearly subscription also gives you a 26 percent discount. Angie's List has 3 different plans: the Green Plan, the Silver Plan and the Gold Plan. The Green Plan contains all of the basic features in Angie's List including Nationwide Coverage, Angie’s List Magazine, top-quality service providers and highly trusted ratings. The Silver Plan includes all of the Green Plan features but contains exclusive discounts, Project Financing and Scheduling, and a Blue Book Pricing Guide. The Gold Plan, contains all of the Silver Plan and Green Plan features but more! It has live member care support, a complaint resolution process, exclusive eCommerce savings, an emergency service line, a handy chat and Insta-Hire. Although the Green Plan is free, the Silver Plan($24.99/year) and Gold Plan($99.99/year) contain more features which is beneficial to active users.

How to Use Angie's List

To make money on Angie’s List you must first find a local offer and buy with the confidence of upfront pricing. Angie’s List does a great job of keeping fake consumers and reviews out and is BPA Worldwide Certified. Then, you will receive an email receipt with redemption information. Schedule your service and redeem your offer. Finally, finish your project/projects on time and on budget. IT’s that easy. Many have succeeded on Angie’s List and you can too!
