Work Permits.

On this page, you can find a breif overview of all the green card information and regulation relating to immigrant workers.

EB-1 Green Card

For people with strong ablities in the sciences, arts, education, business, or athletics

EB-3 Green Card Permit

Designed for skilled workers with atleast three years of experience, professionals with bachelors, and unskiller workers.

EB-4 Green Card Permit

Immigrants who are religious workers, employees or former employees for the US government abroad, and translators for the US Armed forces.

EB-5 Green Card Permit

Reserved for business investors in a new commercial enterprise that employs at least ten full time US workers.

After receiving your work visa, make sure that your eligibility is on your resume and communicate with your employer on how long you are able to work for. When going to interviews, make sure to have your paperwork in order to prove that you are eligible to work
